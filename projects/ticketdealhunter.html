Ticket Deal Hunter

Description

IN PROGRESS!!!

Stemming from my desire to teach myself webscraping (as well as my tendency to hunt for cheap deals when searching for event tickets), I envisioned a webapp that'd allow me to avoid having to repeatedly check all various ticket reselling services to compare prices. I'm still tinkering with the best way to scrape these websites (legally, of course), but I hope the end result will be a simple tool that allows you to find the 5-10 cheapest ticket options for your desired event.
